Our Story

What happened and what will come...

  • Past
  • Present
  • Future
The Mythical Macaques were part of a larger tribe deep deep in the jungle. The tribe was led by (...) kings and these kings were up to no good. The home of the tribe was getting less livable and the old kings knew this, but didn't share this with the tribe. Everyday the old kings gave the tribe the feeling that everything was fine, while they knew it wasn't. One day everyone went to sleep, except the old kings. They executed their plan to start a new smaller tribe where all the supplies would be theirs and they wouldn't need to do anything anymore. Whilst the most of the tribe were asleep the old kings and their allies took all the supplies of the tribe and ran away to begin their own new journey.
When the tribe woke up they didn't believe their eyes. The treasury empty, supplies to live of gone and the worst part: the chance to survive very low. The tribe was so desperate and hopeless. Their home area was not sustainable enough for everyone and they had to make a decision on how they would survive the crisis. But how? ⏳ Luckily enough not a long time ago a group of 305 babies were born and these babies were now grown enough to take care of themselves. The tribe decided that this group of young tribe members were their only hope in finding a new place to live. They knew that this group would face a lot of surprise in their journey, but they were their only hope to survive and that is where our journey begins.
Before the group was sent out they gave them a name as a group. They called them the "Mythical Macaques" because if they would survive everyone would believe it would be a myth. The oldest tribe members sent them on their way with some of the gathered supplies and this is where our journey begins. 🌪️ Will we succeed together as a tight community or will we fail in our journey to survive? Wanna be part of this journey and overcome the challenges that lie ahead? Join us now!
